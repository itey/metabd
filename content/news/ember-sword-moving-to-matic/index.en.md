To solve scaling issues and gas fee problems on the Ethereum blockchain, Bright Star Studios has decided to move Ember Sword to the Matic Network. The developers told in their recent Embercast podcast that Matic and the services they offer will be enough. However, if issues will arise Bright Star Studios has a backup plan with their own sidechain.
